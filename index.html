MENU CARD

STARTERS

Starters List :-

Chilli-Chicken

Chilli Chicken is a popular Indo-Chinese dish that’s known for its bold flavors, spicy heat, and savory goodness. It typically features marinated, bite-sized pieces of chicken that are either fried or sautéed to achieve a crispy exterior while remaining juicy inside.

Malai Chicken Tikka

Malai Chicken Tikka is a creamy, succulent chicken starter that’s both rich in flavor and incredibly tender. Unlike the spicier Tandoori Chicken Tikka, Malai Chicken Tikka is mild, with a creamy marinade, This dish is highly popular in India.

Tandoori Chicken Tikka

Tandoori Chicken Tikka is a classic Indian appetizer known for its vibrant color, smoky aroma, and bold flavors. This dish is made from boneless chunks of chicken marinated in a spicy, yogurt-based mixture and cooked in a tandoor (traditional clay oven) at high temperatures.

Crispy Chilli Chicken Bites

Crispy Chilli Chicken Bites are a popular, bite-sized version of Chilli Chicken, ideal for those who love spicy, crunchy, and flavorful appetizers. These crispy bites are made with small pieces of marinated chicken that are battered, fried to golden perfection, and then tossed in a spicy, tangy sauce.

Spicy Chicken Lollipops

Spicy Chicken Lollipops are a popular Indo-Chinese appetizer known for their unique presentation and bold, spicy flavor. Made by shaping chicken wings or drumettes to resemble lollipops, these bite-sized treats are coated in a flavorful marinade.

BIRYANIS

Biriyani's List :-

Hyderabadi Dum Biryani

Hyderabadi Dum Biryani is one of the most iconic and beloved dishes from Hyderabad, India. Renowned for its rich flavors, aromatic spices, and distinctive cooking method, it represents the culinary heritage of the region. Here's a deep dive into this dish.

Ambur Biryani

Ambur Biryani is a famous South Indian biryani known for its distinctive flavor and preparation method. Originating from the town of Ambur in Tamil Nadu, it holds a special place in Indian culinary traditions, especially as part of the Tamil Nadu Muslim community's cuisine. Here’s an overview.

Mughlai Biryani

Mughlai Biryani is a luxurious and aromatic rice dish that originated in the royal kitchens of the Mughal Empire. It represents the fusion of Persian, Middle Eastern, and Indian culinary traditions, making it a rich and flavorful feast fit for royalty.

Kashmiri Biryani

Kashmiri Biryani is a delightful and aromatic variation of biryani that incorporates the flavors and culinary traditions of Kashmir. Known for its subtle sweetness, vibrant colors, and fragrant spices, it stands out as a milder and more unique take on the traditional biryani.

Keema Biryani

Keema Biryani is a flavorful and aromatic rice dish made with minced meat (keema) and fragrant spices. It is a delicious twist on traditional biryani, offering a unique texture and rich taste due to the finely minced meat blending seamlessly with the rice.

SPECIALS

Our Special's List :-

Garlic Naan

Garlic Naan is a popular Indian flatbread made with a dough that is enriched with yogurt and sometimes milk, giving it a soft and fluffy texture. What sets garlic naan apart is its fragrant topping of fresh garlic and butter, which gives it a distinctive, aromatic flavor.

Kachumber Salad

Kachumber Salad is a refreshing and light Indian salad made with a simple mix of raw vegetables and a tangy dressing. It’s a popular side dish that provides a cooling balance to the spiciness of Indian meals, especially dishes like biryanis, curries, or grilled meats.

Masala Fries

Masala Fries are a flavorful twist on the classic French fries, infused with a blend of aromatic Indian spices. This dish combines the crispy texture of golden, fried potatoes with a zesty, spicy seasoning, creating a mouthwatering snack or side dish that pairs well with many meals.

MOCKTAILS

Mocktail's List :-

Virgin Mojito

Virgin Mojito is a refreshing and tangy non-alcoholic drink that’s perfect for quenching your thirst on a hot day or as a lively mocktail to complement a meal. It’s a twist on the classic mojito, with all the vibrant flavors intact but without any alcohol.

Watermelon Slush

Watermelon Slush is a refreshing, icy drink made from pureed watermelon and ice, offering a naturally sweet and hydrating experience. This mocktail is the perfect beverage for hot summer days or as a refreshing treat any time of the year.

Citrus Cooler

Citrus Cooler is a vibrant, zesty, and refreshing mocktail that combines the tangy flavors of various citrus fruits with the effervescence of soda water or sparkling water. It's a perfect thirst-quencher, offering a balance of sweetness and tartness, ideal for hot days or as a revitalizing drink to complement spicy or heavy meals.

Peach Iced Tea

Peach Iced Tea is a delicious and refreshing cold beverage that combines the natural sweetness of ripe peaches with the crisp, slightly bitter flavor of brewed tea. This drink is perfect for hot days, offering a cool, fruity twist on traditional iced tea.

Strawberry Mojito

Strawberry Mojito is a refreshing and fruity twist on the classic Virgin Mojito, blending the sweet taste of ripe strawberries with the tangy lime and refreshing mint. It's a perfect mocktail for those who enjoy fruity beverages with a zesty, slightly sweet, and cooling flavor.

Ice-cream's List :-

Vanilla Bean

Vanilla Bean Ice Cream is a classic and timeless flavor that is beloved for its smooth, creamy texture and the subtle, aromatic taste of real vanilla. Unlike standard vanilla ice cream, vanilla bean ice cream is made with actual vanilla beans, which give it a more complex, natural flavor.

Chocolate Fudge Brownie

Chocolate Fudge Brownie Ice Cream is a rich, decadent dessert that combines the velvety smoothness of chocolate ice cream with chunks of fudgy brownie pieces, making it a perfect treat for chocolate lovers. The ice cream is indulgent, with layers of chocolate flavor.

Strawberry Cheesecake

Strawberry Cheesecake Ice Cream is a creamy, indulgent dessert that combines the rich, tangy flavor of cheesecake with the sweetness of fresh strawberries. This flavor captures the essence of a classic strawberry cheesecake but in the form of ice cream.

Cookies and Cream

Salted Caramel Ice Cream is a rich, indulgent dessert that beautifully balances the sweet, creamy flavor of traditional caramel with a touch of savory sea salt. This flavor has gained immense popularity due to its sophisticated taste, which combines both sweet and salty notes.

DESSERTS

Dessert's List :-

Gulab Jamun

Gulab Jamun is a beloved and indulgent traditional Indian dessert that is often served at celebrations, festivals, and special occasions. These soft, round dumplings are made from a dough primarily composed of milk solids, flour, and ghee.

Caramel Custard

Caramel Custard, also known as Crème Caramel, is a classic, smooth, and velvety dessert that combines the richness of custard with the sweetness of caramel. This timeless treat is loved for its delicate texture and the balance between the creamy custard and the deep.

Chocolate Lava Cake

Chocolate Lava Cake, also known as a Molten Chocolate Cake, is a decadent dessert that combines the richness of chocolate cake with a warm, gooey center that flows out like molten lava when cut. It is a beloved treat for chocolate lovers, offering an indulgent balance of cake and chocolatey goodness.

Tiramisu

Tiramisu is a classic Italian dessert that has gained worldwide popularity for its rich, creamy texture and delightful layers of coffee-soaked ladyfingers, mascarpone cheese, and cocoa. The name "Tiramisu" means "pick me up" in Italian.

Kulfi

Kulfi is a traditional Indian frozen dessert, often considered a type of ice cream but with a denser and creamier texture. Unlike regular ice cream, which is churned to incorporate air, kulfi is made by slow-cooking milk and reducing it to a thick, creamy consistency.
